Placement Portal - Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible to participate in the placement process, you must meet the following criteria:

  • **Branch:** You must be a final year student enrolled in one of the following branches:
    • Computer Engineering
    • Information Technology
    • Electronics and Communication Engineering
    • Electrical Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering
    • Civil Engineering
  • **Minimum CGPA:** You must have a minimum CGPA of 7.0 (or equivalent percentage) throughout your program.
  • **No Active Backlogs:** You must not have any active backlogs (pending courses) at the time of placement.
  • **Disciplinary Record:** You must have a clean disciplinary record with no major disciplinary actions against you.

**Note:** This is a general overview of the eligibility criteria. Companies participating in the placement process may have additional specific requirements. Please refer to the individual company profiles for detailed information.
